Immerse yourself in the atmosphere of different cultures in our ethno-cafe

Our cafe is saturated with the spirit and heritage of different ethnic cultures. We carefully select the scenery, music and menu to convey the atmosphere of different parts of the world.

In our interior you can find unique handicrafts, art and artifacts, each of which tells its own story.

However, "Ethno" is not only a place to admire the beauties of different cultures, but also to enjoy the tastes and aromas of their cuisines. Our menu offers a wealth of flavors from around the world, from aromatic curries to tender kebabs. We use only fresh and natural ingredients to offer our guests a real culinary experience.

But our main pride is our team. We are a friendly and talented team that share common values ​​and passion for what we do.

We are glad to welcome every guest in our cafe and make his visit unforgettable.
